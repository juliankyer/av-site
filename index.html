ÆVOLO

CYCLING TEAM

ÆVOLO's aim in cycling is to be a touchstone our riders use

not only for their athletic careers, but for life. By teaching

them how to look at both success and failure objectively, and

without any judgement or blame, by not allowing willful ignorance

and relying exclusively on hope, we teach our riders how to look

in the mirror without fear or regret.

Honesty and understanding builds not only better bike racers,

but better human beings.

Director Mike Creed
